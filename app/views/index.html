Documents and document upload Experimental

We've built a repository for holding documents. We are looking at ways in which these documents could be uploaded and surfaced throughout the service.

We envisage that documents could be uploaded within the context of other tasks - such as adding a court case through the Remand and Sentencing - but we've also been looking at ways in which users could upload and access documents through the hub.

Snapshots of what the hub looks like at each stage of release

We need to consider what the hub might look like a different stages of release.

Adjustments will be rolled out around March but not to all prisons currently using the CRDS service, this might be the same scenario when it comes time to release Remand and Sentencing.

This means that there may be multiple variations of the hub in different prisons at one time. We need to account for this in the design.

Changes in this version

This is the first version of the hub design during this new phase of work.

As we kick-off this work, we stripped out the majority of the previous design features (for now), with an aim to carry out some UR to identify the most important features to focus on MVP. We then carried out a card-sorting activity with users, primarily from reception prisons.

We'll use this UR to inform our initial designs for build and to help us target specific features to research more in-depth in the next round.
